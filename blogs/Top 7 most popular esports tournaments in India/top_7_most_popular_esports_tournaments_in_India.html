Top 7 most popular esports tournaments in India

Date:17/10/2023

India’s esports scene has grown exponentially over the past decade, with aspiring and enthusiastic gamers fueling the rise of the competitive gaming industry in the country. While esports tournaments have been in the foray within the country since the start of the century, it is in the past decade that it has gained the surge of prominence which it is currently enjoying.

These tournaments attract several players from all over the country with their exciting and lucrative prize pools and serious opportunities for gamers to test their skills within a competitive environment. This article will explore the top 7 esports tournaments that dominate the competitive gaming scene in India.

Skyesports Championship

Skyesports hosted one of the most highly anticipated Skyesports Championship 3.0, the third installment of their Championship series, with a staggering prize pool of 26,00,000 INR. With games like Brawl Stars, Clash of Clans, BGMI, and Valorant represented, this tournament was a standout in India’s esports calendar. The astounding total prize pool of 55,00,000 INR made Skyesports Championship 3.0 one of the biggest esports competitions in the nation. It seeks to enthral both players and viewers.

ESL India Premiership

The Indian gaming community eagerly awaits each edition of ESL India Premiership – one of the most significant esports tournament series in Asia Pacific region- with baited breath every year! With multiple seasons featuring action packed games such as CS:GO, Dota 2 and Clash Royale, its no wonder why this event attracts the best teams in the country. The competition provides a platform for talented players to showcase their skills in front of a global audience. With huge cash prizes and top tier production quality that is on display the ESL India Premiership is undoubtedly one of India’s premier esports competitions.

PUBG Mobile India Series

PUBG Mobile India Series stands tall as one of Indias premier esports tournaments dedicated entirely to showcasing high octane battles featuring gamers from around the country competing against each other for substantial prizes. Known for its highly addictive gameplay mechanics this popular battle royale game has truly made an impact on Indian gamers nationally resulting in impressive participation numbers since day one. Its immense success can be attributed not only to its massive player base but also due to its overall remarkable appeal within the Indian esports community.

The Indian Esports League (IEL)

The India Esports League is a well-known tournament series that features games like Call of Duty, Dota 2, and CS:GO. Professional teams can use it as a stage to display their talents and contend for big prize money. With its well-organized league format, thrilling playoffs, and live broadcasts, the IEL draws a sizable audience and promotes the expansion of esports in India.

ESL One Mumbai

The ESL One Mumbai Dota 2 competition is a well-known event that takes place in Mumbai, India. Top-tier Dota 2 teams from all around the world participate in this renowned event to fight for a sizable prize pool. ESL One Mumbai has firmly established itself as a significant esports event in India, gaining both local and international attention because to its great production value, enthusiastic audience, and furious competition.

UCypher

UCypher, which was first hosted by MTV India, concentrates on several titles, such as CS:GO, Dota 2, and Tekken 7. The way in which professional esports athletes and celebrities establish teams and compete against one another makes UCypher unique. This fusion of entertainment and competitive gaming has become extremely successful, drawing a large audience and introducing esports to a wider public.

DreamHack India

DreamHack, a world-renowned esports organization expanded its reach in India for the first time with DreamHack Mumbai. Gaming lovers, professional gamers, and business professionals come together for this festival-style event. CS:GO, Dota 2, and Super Smash Bros. Ultimate are just a few of the esports competitions that are part of DreamHack India. DreamHack India has established itself as a hub for both esports enthusiasts and casual gamers because to its energetic environment, costume competitions, and interactive exhibitions.

These top seven competitions demonstrate the scope of the competitive gaming ecosystem in India, where the esports industry is flourishing. From massive tournaments like the ESL India Premiership and India Gaming Carnival to globally renowned events like ESL One Mumbai, the esports competitive scene in India is seemingly evergrowing from hereon.
